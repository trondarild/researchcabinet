# Projects

## Project Template

When starting a new writing project, create a sub-page with:

```markdown
---
title: Project Title
status: outlining | drafting | revising | under review | accepted
target: journal or publisher
deadline: YYYY-MM-DD
---

## Central Claim
One sentence: what this paper argues.

## Why It Matters
What gap in the literature it fills, or what position it challenges.

## Outline
1. Section 1 — ...
2. Section 2 — ...
3. Conclusion — ...

## Key Texts to Engage
- Author (Year) — reason for engagement
- Author (Year) — reason for engagement

## Open Questions
What we haven't figured out yet.

## Drafts
Link to draft files or note version history here.
```
