# Seminars

Reading lists, session prep notes, and post-session summaries for seminars, reading groups, and courses.

## Active Seminars

Create a sub-page per seminar or reading group with its full reading list and session notes.

## Session Template

```markdown
## Session N — Author, Title (Chapter/Section)

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Leader:** Name

### Reading Summary
What the assigned reading argues, in 3–5 sentences.

### Discussion Questions
1. Question 1
2. Question 2
3. Question 3

### Key Moments from Discussion
- Point raised by X: ...
- Unresolved tension: ...

### Follow-up Reading
Texts that came up and should be read next.
```

## Seminar Planning Notes

- Keep reading lists realistic — depth over breadth
- Primary texts before secondary literature where possible
- Leave room in the final session for synthesis and open questions
- Session summaries are the most valuable long-term artifact — write them the same day
