# Lab

Research group operations, goals, and shared norms.

## Sub-sections

- **Goals** — research objectives for the current period
- **Team** — members, areas of focus, contact info
- **Grants** — active and pending funding
- **Norms** — working agreements, writing practices, seminar conventions

## Research Goals Template

| Goal | Metric | Target | Status |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| Submit paper | Submission | 1 paper | In progress |
| Complete reading group | Sessions | 8 sessions | 3/8 done |
| Develop new argument | Draft argument map | 1 complete map | Outlining |

## Lab Norms

Document your group's working agreements here. Things worth writing down:

- **Feedback culture** — how you give and receive criticism on drafts
- **Seminar norms** — how sessions are run, what counts as good discussion
- **Writing practices** — version control for drafts, shared document conventions
- **Citation conventions** — house style, reference manager in use
- **Meeting rhythm** — how often, what format, who leads
