## Project Template

When starting a new project, create a sub-page with:

1. **Objective** — one sentence stating what you are testing or building
2. **Background** — relevant prior work and motivation
3. **Methods** — experimental or computational approach
4. **Timeline** — key milestones with dates
5. **Success criteria** — how you will know the project succeeded
