# Example Research Lab

This is a sample research lab Cabinet — a starting point showing how to organize a research group's knowledge base with AI agents.

## Why This Example Works

- Research labs generate large amounts of context (papers, protocols, data, decisions) that's hard to keep organized.
- AI agents can review literature, track project milestones, and draft reports on a schedule.
- The knowledge base becomes the shared memory between researchers and AI assistants.

## Lab Modules

- [[projects]] — Active research projects and milestones
- [[literature]] — Paper summaries and literature reviews
- [[protocols]] — Experimental and analysis protocols
- [[lab]] — Team info, goals, and lab operations

## Active AI Team

- Research Director — strategic alignment and project prioritization
- Researcher — literature review and hypothesis generation
- Lab Manager — operational reviews and resource coordination
- Grants Manager — funding tracking and budget oversight

The example agents are paused by default. Activate them in the Agents panel and customize their personas to match your lab's focus.
