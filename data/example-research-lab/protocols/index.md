# Protocols

Standard operating procedures and experimental protocols.

## Conventions

- Each protocol lives as its own sub-page
- Include version number and last-modified date in the frontmatter
- Major changes should be reviewed and approved before use
- Link to the paper or source that validated the protocol

## Protocol Template

```markdown
---
title: Protocol Name
version: 1.0
validated: YYYY-MM-DD
source: DOI or reference
---

## Purpose
What this protocol achieves.

## Materials
- Item 1
- Item 2

## Steps
1. Step one
2. Step two

## Notes and Common Pitfalls
- Watch out for X
- If Y happens, do Z

## Version History
- v1.0 — initial version
```
